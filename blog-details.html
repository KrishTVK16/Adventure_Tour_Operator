Complete Packing List for Patagonia

Everything you need for the "W" trek.

By Jane Doe • Jan 20, 2026

Patagonia is notorious for its rapidly changing weather. You might experience all four seasons in a single day. Here is how to prepare.

The Essentials

1. Waterproof Shell: This is non-negotiable. Look for Gore-Tex or similar technology.

2. Layers: Merino wool base layers are your best friend. They keep you warm even when wet.

Footwear

Your boots should be broken in before you arrive. Blisters can ruin a trip faster than rain.

Pro Tip: Bring extra socks. More than you think you need.

Conclusion

With the right gear, Patagonia is a paradise. Without it, it can be a challenge. Pack smart!
